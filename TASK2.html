Grid Container With Answers:

1. What does fr stand for?
fr means "fractional unit". It divides available space into equal parts.
2. How many columns are created?
3 columns are created using grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr 1fr.
3. What happens when screen size is reduced?
The grid shrinks horizontally and tries to fit within the screen.
4. Do items overflow or adjust?
Items adjust inside the columns and reduce width instead of overflowing.
5. Why is fr better than px for layouts?
fr adjusts automatically with screen size, while px stays fixed.
